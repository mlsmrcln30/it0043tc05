Inori Yurzuriha

Void Genome

Inori Yuzuriha (楪 いのり, Yuzuriha Inori) was the female protagonist of Guilty Crown and a member of the resistance guerrilla group called "Funeral Parlor." She was the voice of the internet artist Egoist. Inori was a beautiful girl with light pink hair that ombres into hot pink and is tied into pigtails, she tied her hair using two tubes, and had a small red clip on the left side of her face. Inori had red eyes, she was about 166cm tall with small glossed lips. She looked very fragile, having a very slender figure and thin legs. Her usual fighting attire is a red leotard with the middle mostly cut out, red-orange spoilers with flowers on the side and high red gloves. She also wears a black short dress with white lining on the bottom and red ribbons on the bottom, which she wears it when she is not fighting. When attending school, she wears the standard school uniform. She also wears a white outfit in place of her red flowery leotard outfit.

He was known for his strong leadership and determination in fighting against oppressive forces. Gai has spiky black hair and piercing blue eyes. Ayase Shinomiya

Wheelchair Expert

Ayase Shinomiya (四ノ宮 綾生, Shinomiya Ayase) was a prominent member of the resistance group "Funeral Parlor" in Guilty Crown. Despite being confined to a wheelchair due to a spinal injury, Ayase plays a crucial role in supporting the group through her technological expertise and unwavering determination. Ayase has long, dark hair and gentle brown eyes. She is often seen wearing a sophisticated and stylish wheelchair designed to accommodate her needs. Despite her physical limitations, Ayase is portrayed as strong-willed and resilient, contributing significantly to the group's efforts.

Yuu

Void Genome

Yuu (ユウ, Yū) is a key character in Guilty Crown and a member of the resistance group "Funeral Parlor." Although initially mysterious and enigmatic, Yuu plays a significant role in the unfolding events of the series, often providing valuable insights and assistance to the main characters. Yuu has a calm and composed demeanor, with silver hair and piercing violet eyes. He often wears a dark, hooded cloak that conceals much of his appearance, adding to his air of mystery. Despite his reserved nature, Yuu demonstrates a deep understanding of the conflicts surrounding him and proves to be a reliable ally to his comrades in Funeral Parlor.
